Our Story

Bridging the Gap in Aquatic Demand

Founded on the principle of sustainability, RB Farms is dedicated to addressing the growing demand for high-quality fish in Ghana and beyond. We combine modern aquaculture techniques with a deep respect for the environment to produce healthy, delicious fish.

Our core mission extends beyond business; we are committed to empowering our local community by creating stable employment opportunities, especially for youth and women, fostering economic growth and food security for the region.

Our Harvest

Fresh from Our Waters

Premium Tilapia

Renowned for its mild, sweet flavor and firm texture. Our Tilapia is raised in nutrient-rich waters, ensuring a high-quality product perfect for a variety of culinary delights.

Hearty Catfish

A beloved staple, our Catfish are known for their distinctive, savory taste. Farmed with meticulous care, they provide an excellent source of protein and are central to many traditional dishes.

Future: Fish Feed

We are expanding into fish feed production to ensure a fully sustainable cycle, providing optimal nutrition for our fish and offering quality feed to other farms in the region.

How We Do It

Our Sustainable Process

1. Eco-Friendly Hatchery

Our process begins in a state-of-the-art hatchery where we ensure the highest survival rates and genetic quality, without the use of harmful chemicals.

2. Pure Water Source

Our fish are raised in pristine, earthen ponds fed by natural water sources. We continuously monitor water quality to mimic their natural habitat.

3. Organic Nutrition

We are committed to providing our fish with high-protein, organic feed, ensuring they are healthy, nutritious, and free from antibiotics.

4. Ethical Harvesting

We use humane harvesting techniques to minimize stress on the fish, which preserves the quality, texture, and flavor of the final product.
