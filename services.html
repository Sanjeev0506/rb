Our Services

Comprehensive Solutions for Your Aquaculture Needs

Catfish Sale

We provide healthy, fast-growing catfish, raised in optimal conditions to ensure premium taste and quality. Available for both individual consumers and commercial distributors.

Tilapia Sale

Start your farm with the best. We offer high-quality catfish and tilapia fingerlings that are bred for rapid growth and strong disease resistance, ensuring a successful harvest.
